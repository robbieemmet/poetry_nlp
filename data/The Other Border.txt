The Other Border

It's a cold night
in the empty barn, full of holes,
that sky so black and chill
it seems the ocean crashed on you.

No dairy cows here anymore,
and the bees are settled for the winter -
this is no land of milk and honey
like they told you.

It is a land of sugar snow
and maple syrup.
The cold never tasted
so sweet to you,

but after the fires in the north woods
and on the southern border,
what else is there
but retreat for you?
