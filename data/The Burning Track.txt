The Burning Track

Your music -
this will be my memory,

the pre-electric hum
of chorusing cicadas

buzzing with the air
above the gravel bed.

What I have learned
waiting for trains

in the sweet smell
of little-used rights of way,

which smell like power lines
and railroad lines

and people thinking
ōI don't careö -

what I have learned
is that you can wait

and wait
and still get burned.
