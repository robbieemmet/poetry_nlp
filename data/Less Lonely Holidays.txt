Less Lonely Holidays

I miss the less lonely holidays -
no lights in the trees,
no curtains of light in the sky,
but fireworks put there for summer nights
ephemeral as fireflies,

when the sun kept you company,
and the close of each day
brought a beautiful sunset
to take your breath away,

and even if the sunsets now
are beautiful too,
they're made lesser by
not sharing them with you.

I'm alone in the dark.
I'm cold and in pain,
but I'd give up summer
to see you again.
