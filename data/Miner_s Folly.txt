Miner's Folly

Not long ago, before the glow
of eerie northern lights,
there were men here, and not just deer,
deep in Ash Canyon's heights.

Here is a bear, a work bench there
and rumors, still, of gold,
but only a fool would face the cruel
and empty, bitter cold

for fortunes lost and biting frost
and gold that you can't eat.
A better goal is finding coal
and burning it for heat.

So go away and try to stay
in kinder settlements.
What's left behind is, you will find,
the other elements.
