Camping at Mystery Lake

Remember swimming in the lake
when it was warm and blue?
The best part of that summer
was the time I spent with you.

We fished out on the tranquil lake,
letting our lines sink deep.
At night, safe in our cabin,
the wolves sang us to sleep.

Now I'm burning memories.
The logbook feeds the flame.
I wish you were here with me
even though I burned your name.
