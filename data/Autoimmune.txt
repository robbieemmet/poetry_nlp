Autoimmune

It's like these arteries
were built to be clogged.

Each cell treats every other
as the enemy.

Antibody
takes on a whole new meaning.

Who can stop a body
at war with itself?

Everyone on this road
is a snake eating themselves.

Point A to Point B
is always a circle -

you go forward
only to go back again

and the road to hell
is a track paved

with weapons of individual destruction,
thoughtless, lifeless, eating themselves.
