Alga, the God of Existence Value

When I'm blue or I am lonely,
on the road and missing you,
I think of places I may never
see that I appreciate.

She glows then in a wisp of thought,
fluttering on wings of dreams
between the places, people, things
that I have read about in books,
the lone loon guides, the sacred shrines,
the old stone walls and babbling brooks

and me and every dreamer who
knows you don't have to see a place
or meet a kith or a touch a thing
for it to enrich your life.

She is the love of things unseen.
There is no price tag on her grace.
