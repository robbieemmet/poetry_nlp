Smoke in Unexpected Mountains

I saw signs of hospitality
deep in Hushed River Valley.
Or is it hospitality?

Help and welcome look so similar
written on the frosty air -
signals drifting everywhere.

What the smoke is not is fairly clear -
it's too wet for brush fires here,
that's too much smoke for cooking deer.

So what could this signal be instead?
Is it food and a warm bed,
or a cry from the near-dead?
