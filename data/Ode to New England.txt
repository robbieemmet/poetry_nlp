Ode to New England

When I die,
you don't need to bury my heart here.
It's everywhere already in this land.

I hope I can deserve this place,
the villages, the open space
between the places I knew when I was young.

From Greenwich to Penobscot,
from Champlain to the sea,
I am part of New England
and New England's part of me.

No matter how it changes
this will always be the same:
New England called my name.
