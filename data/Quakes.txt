Quakes

The memories of earlier are fading,
shaken to bits by some great omnivore.
The river days were easy, long, and hopeful.
The banks were steady, in that time before.

Now rivers are the only things like highways,
and highways move and crumble, buck and flow.
They flipped the hourglass, and now time pours sideways.
The years burn fast, the minutes trickle slow.
