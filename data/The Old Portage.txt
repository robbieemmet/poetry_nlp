The Old Portage

Before, you had to walk
with your canoe over your head.
Now the lakes are frozen
and the voyageurs are dead.

They thought they needed fur back then?
I never thought I'd wear
a coat I made with fish hooks
and the hide of the Old Bear.

These woods were full of outlaws
and these woods were full of dreams.
Now they're full of silence
and a thousand frozen streams.

The road has changed. There's no portage.
Now you can walk or ski
across the forlorn muskeg
to the bleak and swirling sea.
