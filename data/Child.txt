Child

I guess
I never stopped being afraid of the dark,
never looked Death in the face
and said “I love you”.

It's one thing
when you meet someone with
metastatic cancer
and tell yourself,
“This is the last time I will see them.”

It's another thing
when you don't know what's wrong,
or what will be wrong one day,
when you don't want to lose them,
but staring you in the face
is the fact that you will.

I guess
if life were a game of hide and seek,
I'd think I was good at hiding.
That doesn't mean hiding is good.
