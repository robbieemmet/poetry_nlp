So Close It Could Be Ours

A shining beacon placed high on a hill
has that much farther down that it can fall.
The planet Rhen Var brought on its deep chill
when the bold leaders started the last fall.
The summers had been hot, getting more so.
The engineers decided to play god
and blocked the sun, blanketed fields with snow.
Prayers can't change our fate when we play god.
This planet may be on the Outer Rim,
but its fate is so close it could be ours.
The path to dodge catastrophe is slim.
Even the moon can melt wax wings like ours.
When ears close up and history turns cold,
burning history books won't stop the cold.
