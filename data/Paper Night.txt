Paper Night

My nerves are on fire tonight.
The air is so tense
I think it could rip in half
at any moment.

I crumple up the cobwebs
in a ball
and try to sleep again.

And out in the woods
the night is quiet -
that isn't right,

it should be buzzing
with frogs and crickets
this time of year.

Oh great, another reason for fear.
I crumple up the cobwebs
and try again.
