The Waterfall's Broken Heart

Listen well, and you will hear
the waterfall a-pining.
There is a tale that started here
of lovers intertwining.

One used to come and take a drink
between his Swarming strolls.
The other used to sit and think
beside the quiet pools.

They saw each other, late one day,
across the waters teeming
with dragonflies buzzing away,
their wings and bodies gleaming

like stars that sparkled in their eyes
when the kith saw each other.
Each one was quick to recognize
the other as his lover.

Their laughter gave them music chills.
Their passion made them stronger.
They roamed the pools and blooming hills,
wishing the long days longer.

When the war came, they hid together
in a nearby cave,
but magic was a force too strong
for this to keep them safe.

A wizard from a rival clan
found the kith together,
and with a wicked spell began
to part the two forever.

The kith pledged their eternal bonds
as one turned into stone.
The other melted into the ponds
and left his love alone.

And to this day a cold stone wall
stands sentry where it started,
and by his side a waterfall
forever broken-hearted.
