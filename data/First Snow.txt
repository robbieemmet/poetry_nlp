First Snow

Forces -

Just, the wind swirling snow
up between the mills

and cold air pushing in
the loft windows.

The wind settles,
and for a moment

the snow falls
the way snow is supposed to fall.

Meanwhile the central air keeps humming
up against the cold -

this too is settling,
the agreement

that it is cold out there
and warm in here

and for just this moment
that feels worth any price.
