Suh, A God of Stories in Snow

When the road is closed by snow,
not the home it usually is,

when you den or nest
in any place with open doors

that you can bar against the cold,
that's where he goes.

You and your friends
sit by the fire

and he sits too,
as silent as the snow

falling outside, and listens.
In the gleaming day

he tells a story of his own,
printing a hunt or feast

or romp or lonely journey,
voiceless but for snow.
