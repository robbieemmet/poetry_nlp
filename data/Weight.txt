Weights

They are the ladder
from story to stories,
from person to population.

They are the voice
crying, “I am here,
and so are these others!”

The small ones
speak for themselves, holding up
the food pyramid with their weight.

The large ones
teeter at the top, throwing their voices,
shining a light over shadowy lands.

Weights are like prices on diamonds -
the brighter they shine,
the harder they were to find.
