A Recipe Left Behind

Saturday morning used to mean something different,
a time of rest, with maybe the odd chore,
a welcome change of pace on Great Bear Island,
a thing to celebrate. That was before.

I found this simple recipe for pancakes.
I don't have butter, though. Will bear fat do?
Do I have time to make a childhood favorite
when every day I struggle to get through?

Enough. The days are long, the nights are endless.
There's no time like the present to live well.
I'll make the pancakes. Maybe for a moment
I can forget I live in frozen hell.
