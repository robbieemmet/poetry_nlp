Discowvery

Cows in space, the new frontier.
Are there any livestock here?
Each feature pulls a different thread -
a dairy barn, a livestock shed,

figurative and literal trails
(no brooms are tied to these cows’ tails)
so we can see them on the hoof
or from the shape of a building roof.

That's how we'd like this stuff to work
but every cow farm has its quirk.
Some cows get corn, and others hay.
Some cows aren't present every day.

The cooling cow trail might grow hot
when we start looking where they’re not.
No mountain slopes, no fields of wheat,
no shopping mall, no city street

would be a place a cow could go.
They live in cowabungalows!
We'll find the cows, we'll get it right.
We'll find with them with a satellite.

We'll find them in a database.
No cow’s too small to leave no trace.
And when we find them, we'll cowculate
the cows in every single state!
