The Good Moth-Tender

The good moth-tender sits alone
on a chilly night.
He reads the notes that people send
by dripping candlelight.

He hears from people struggling
to get their daily bread.
He hears from people mourning
their new and long-lost dead.

He takes them all into his heart
with warm and starry eyes.
He sends advice and good thoughts
in his comforting replies.
