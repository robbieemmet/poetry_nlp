Coyotes

Thunder, or the roaring city.
Ambulances, or coyotes wailing.
I'm not sure the walls we built
mean anything,

not when we can bend so far
we break into a whole new self,
king of a strange land
that we don't know
and we don't want.

I'm not sure the walls we built
mean anything,
not when our cries in the night
sound so similar,

not when we are desert dwellers
in the dark woods,
and we were never comfortable,
never not forced to be resilient,
never not alone.
