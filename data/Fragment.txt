Fragment

At dawn on April 8th, there will be
a new moon, an eclipse,
and a breaking dawn.

One of two things will be true:
either vampires will move into town,
or a hole will be made in the world

and echoes of times past will pour out,
dinosaur bellows and shouts
in conquered languages.

Extinction events
feel like holes in the world -
everything flies into the dark spot

where the sun was,
leaving the world darker and emptier
than it was,

except this time when the moon moves
and the sun comes out again,
the birds don't sing again.
