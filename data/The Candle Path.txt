The Candle Path

Forest trails, bathed in light,
guide me on my way tonight.
Lead me to a place that I can
call my home.

Candle-gods, guide me well,
over hill and into dell,
on the road to somewhere I can
call my home.

Guided by the brightest star,
I have traveled north so far
I'm not sure how much farther north
I can roam.

In this forest cloaked in snow
and a thousand candles’ glow,
will I find a place that I can
call my home?
