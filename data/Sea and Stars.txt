Sea and Stars

When you drown,
when you go down like the ships broke up
or disappeared out on the lakes and seas
on darker days,
you cannot count the time in breaths.

When you see stars,
the years stretch out
like bright yellow mornings and wondrous nights,
and you see into the past and simpler days,
and you cannot count the time in breaths.

You held your breath
and tried to will it to another.
You've held it since.

They're in the rivers and the seas,
the bright new planets and the endless stars above.
I'd like to believe
that we don't really lose the ones we love.
