The Firelight on the Coast Road

Often she wanders in the maze-like dunes,
guiding the wayward with her firefly.
She's wandered for who knows how many moons,
her bed the beach, her roof the stormy sky.
There is a light inside of her that burns
too bright for others not to feel its rays.
She heals the lost, but deep inside she yearns
for peaceful sleep at night and different days.
The ocean keeps her secrets and her faith.
She is a walking lighthouse for those tossed
on the great waves that lap the shores of Haeth.
She leads them home while she herself is lost.
I hope she comes to rest at last one night
at home where she can save herself some light.
