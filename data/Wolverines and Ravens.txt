Wolverines and Ravens

As I approach the winterkill,
I catch a raven's eye.
I often see them in the trees
wherever corpses lie.

I spot a wolverine, once rare,
now everywhere I go.
They're built for this apocalyptic
world of flesh and snow.

I build a fire against the cold
that steals my steaming breath.
The ravens, wolverines, and me:
our lives are fueled by death.
