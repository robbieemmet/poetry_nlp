It's Not Goodbye, It's See You Later

Some people say that work's not family.
I guess they've never worked at NASS before.
I wanted years (I almost got to three)
but as for friends, I couldn't ask for more.
I didn't know the effort that it takes
to make those all-important estimates.
I didn't know the bounty each farm makes
and brings from field to store and last to plates.
I still don't know, but I do know the skill
it takes to meet the mission every day,
and how, even in this time, even still,
how fine it is to find friends on the way.
And as my time at NASS comes to an end,
I'll think of each of you as a true friend.
