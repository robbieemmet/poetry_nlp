The Shepherds’ Stone Signals of Oxbow Road

Before the shepherds wrote their stories
in these piled stones,
wind and water wrote these tools of writing
into being.

Over and over these stones are overwritten.
If you can count it,
they can be used to show it.
Shepherds passing by will see and know

how many bumbles another shepherd sold,
how many made the trip from Farness to Oxbow,
whether the month of Swarming proved successful
in hatching larvae for the Bumblemeet shows.

The stones tell you what made them
and the bumbles’ stories too -
the ones lost in a fire
marked with pumice stone,

a stone with a crack
for a flock divided,
a rare red pebble for the small god
of safe trips home.
