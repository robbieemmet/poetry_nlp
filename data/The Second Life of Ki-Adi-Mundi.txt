The Second Life of Ki-Adi-Mundi

After Order 66, when Jedi Knights were slain,
he came back as a Force ghost to assist with what remained.

He counseled decommissioned clones, he counseled refugees,
he tried to save Force-sensitives from dark atrocities.

He found Ahsoka Tano and he asked her to forgive
the blindness of the Order, and he urged her now to live

in ways the Force would guide her, even if not Jedi rules,
for they had been manipulated like so many tools,

and the Order had forgotten that only the living Force
and compassion were the best things for a lifetime's proper course.

He saw the Purge coming no less than Master Obi-Wan,
but sometimes bore the blame for all the Jedi who were gone.

In the end he felt compassion, even for the Jedi's foes,
for he knew how darkness sprang from unimaginable woes.

The Order was misguided - Jedi should become attached
to lovers, enemies, all living beings, born or hatched.

There'd be mistakes, betrayal, and more blind religious zeal,
but in the end compassion was the only way to heal.

His two hearts represented what the Jedi Knights did best -
one heart for the Order and another for the rest.
