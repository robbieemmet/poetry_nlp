Winter Left Vacation Early This Year

It felt restless on rooftops,
drifting anxiously, willing itself
to enter a new phase and fly away
south, where it had to fight to be.

Here where it could lie dreaming
on the foaming, crystallizing beach,
winter was happy
but anxious to do more.

It was happy to have made the sap run sweet,
do what winter does so well,
but it had to spread its message
to the world:

slow down,
share and leave yourself
some room to spare,
and above all, give thanks.
