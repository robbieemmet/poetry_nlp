The Well of Shadows

If this is the price
of getting my poetry back,
I don't want it.

I don't know if I could ever really sing
if I didn't need to.
I may never know.

I only feel the words tumble out
when I drink bitter medicine,
the drafts that make me vomit shadows -

a room waiting for a crib,
a closet with a growing pile of toys,
and a picture of something not growing.
