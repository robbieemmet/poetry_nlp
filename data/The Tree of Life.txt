The Tree of Life

Rarest are those treasures
planted for our children
and also ancestors,
trees passing ten ages

seasonless, unchanging,
as all things are changeless
deep in their roots, down where
empires can't reach them.

Work what you will with us -
we are Wookiees, born of
forests and fast-rooted,
no danger undisturbed.

But if you bury us,
you will see us sprouting
while conquerors crumble,
stormtroopers all routing.

Then we will thank the trees,
teachers and towers both,
givers of life lessons,
hardwooded Wookiee homes.
