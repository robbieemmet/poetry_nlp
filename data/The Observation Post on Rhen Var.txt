The Observation Post on Rhen Var

The trooper prowled around the frozen keep,
keeping a sharp eye on the land below.
She had this room to journal, eat and sleep,
but mostly to observe the swirling snow
and scan the frozen face and starry skies
of this dark planet for the enemy,
at the same time avoiding hostile eyes,
here in the margins of the galaxy.
She did patrols, checked in, and made some stew.
She read a novel by a shielded light.
She used her scope to skim the sky and view
a quiet and completely peaceful night.
She settled in to wait and watch the sky,
keeping the rebels safe with her keen eye.
