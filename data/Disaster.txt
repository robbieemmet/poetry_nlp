The unnamed storm hits Hoth like a fist
on a plate, flipping the world.
The horizon line points to the faceless sky,
or so it seems:
at least the position of up
has changed for the hundreds of tauntauns
lying dead.

What could you call it
when the world eats your livelihood?
What could you name a storm
that prevents you from naming it,
stealing your voice, your hands,
until the only communication from you
is your footprints in the snow,
fading as rapidly
as you do?
