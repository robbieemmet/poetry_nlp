The Archive Expeditionary Scholar
Cordova cracked the ice core from the ground.
The crystal rod on the cold planet Rhen Var
held Jedi secrets. Carefully, he held
the ice core, and his new psychometry
found in his studies cast a psychic line
into the depths of cataclysm etched
into the ice core he held carefully.

A Jedi temple, perched among the cliffs
above a chasm, called the Nekkar's Nest.
Calm were the days of scholars and recruits
learning and practicing among the clouds.
Then came the storm, the cold, and columns fell,
collapsing with the bookshelves and the walls.
The empty temple kept the Jedi dead
out of the cold and in the archives of

the Force. Cordova held the history
of this catastrophe in shaking hands.
This was his mission, cataloging things,
no matter if they caused him grief or pain.
His exploration droid, called BD-1,
beeped sympathetically, and climbed his cloak.
Cordova took a last look at the place,
took out his notebook, and began to write.
