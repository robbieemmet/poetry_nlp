The Great Bear Spell

The northern lights are dancing now
in cold and hostile skies.
I have to keep a watch for wolves
but can't avert my eyes.

Each morning is a fight with thirst.
Each night is long and dark.
I think the last thing that I'll hear
will be a hungry bark.

But every day's a painting and
I'm caught up in the spell.
It seems I'm barely living
but I'm barely living well.

My belly's full of bear meat
and my stove is full of wood.
Even if I could get away
I'm not sure that I would.
