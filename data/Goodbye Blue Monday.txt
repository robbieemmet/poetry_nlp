Goodbye Blue Monday

The scene invades my senses like a drug.
The windows sweat with baked goods and warm brew.
The scent envelops me like a warm hug.
Perhaps this chilly Monday won't be blue.
I walk inside and hear the same Neil Young
I always heard over the stereo,
the sound of “Cinnamon Girl” sweetly sung
over a cinnamon-spiced hot cocoa
with chili powder. Warm and happy haze
and buzz of talk and coffee fills the space.
I miss the shop that started off good days.
I miss the town where the air hurt my face.
In every true sense, I'm here, far away,
but in my mind I go there every day.
