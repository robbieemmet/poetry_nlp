Jerr the Livestock Guardian Ant

There is a world we cannot see
with our eyes or even noses.
Chemicals tell a trail -
this is his zone.

He has bonded with the aphids
since his larval stage.
His home is where their herd is,
on the range.

He feeds on aphid honeydew
and knows the price -
a thousand days of restlessness,
a thousand sleepless nights.

But when he keeps the ladybugs
and parasitic wasps away,
he has something sweeter than honeydew -
a grateful family.
