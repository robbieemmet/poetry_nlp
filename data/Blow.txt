Blow

We're probably getting
what everyone else is getting -
a strong and coastal storm.

This is day for indoor games - maybe Amnesia.
I don't question nature
when it gets this large.

The air is cool and clammy -
maybe that earliest
heatstroke of the year.

Maybe for a moment
I will step away from plants
and my New England's old stone walls

and entertain the time bombs in the ocean.
What will summer be like
when sunburn is a state of being

and New England has a monsoon season?
Will Virginia bluebells
sprout beneath the dying maple trees?

How will we retreat from weather
when each house has
its own and lonely harbor?

Even if we live as neighbors,
how will we survive the blows
when even united we fall?
