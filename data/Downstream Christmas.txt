Downstream Christmas

I don't know what will need to change
about the recipe this year.
I know which parts I cannot do,
but not what to do instead.

How many walnut trees were burned
in the only place that grows them here?
Did hurricanes leave sugarcane?
Are the orange trees all dead?

Maple and flour I still have,
though how long still I cannot say.
What I will use to coat the cakes
I simply do not know.

Who knows how many recipes
extreme events have ripped away?
A Christmas without orange cakes
is a Christmas without snow.
