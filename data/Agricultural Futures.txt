Agricultural Futures

Maybe maples,
like a couple married
too long for each other,
will grow less tolerant
and less sweet.

Maybe smoke
will stunt the melons
and the hairy tomato vines.
Maybe bees will pollinate at night
to beat the heat.

Maybe some fields will wither,
maybe even the ones
whose owners deserve it.
Maybe they will sit
like stolen dogs

yielding only to the ones
who owned them first.
Let justice rain,
bring blizzards, prairie fires,
blight the corn, cattle, and hogs.
