Eclipse

As if the day were not too dark before,
the meeting places not too dead and cold.
As if the world has not been long at war:
out with the old, they say, in with the old.
Turning the lights out broke a lot of lives,
but for still more, the lights were never on.
It never worked, who dies and who survives.
The ones you needed most were the first gone.
But humans have survived an endless night,
and somehow made it to the break of day.
We've always found a way to find the light,
even the one that took all lights away.
We all will find the dark and join the dead.
Until that time, we find the light instead.
