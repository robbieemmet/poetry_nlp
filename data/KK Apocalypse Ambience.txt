KK Apocalypse Ambience

You have trained for this.
You know this place.

Fish and fruit
are not too hard to come by.

A raccoon watches your every move
and animals are your neighbors.

You can find interesting things
on the windy beach.

When it snows, the world feels like
it belongs only to you.

Success comes through living
and your home is what you make it.

Is it survival? Is it vacation?
Either way, your world is an island.
