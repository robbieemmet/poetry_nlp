Crevasse

Distance is what you are told.
Across the parking lot you see
the sort of people you never want to be,
self-righteous and cold.

I am not yet out of verse,
only I need a new wellspring
that isn't a geyser to make me sing.
Turbulent water's a curse.

Safe is small and slow,
but look back and see how far you've gone
into this dangerous world of song
and how far you still can go.

Nothing cannot be traversed,
but anything that can be can trap you
neutral to the world, or worse, you
find yourself stuck in reverse.
