The Gift of Warmth

Even at the point of trauma,
there's compassion for the clones.
In the fast-unfolding drama,
they are more than mindless drones.

When they killed the Jedi hero
in the battle and the storm,
for a moment in subzero
howling blizzards, they were warm.

When the clones received the order
and the killing took its course,
in the midst of that disorder
was compassion in the Force.
