Snowliage on the Floating Mountain

It's here at last, the snow,
up on the floating peak,
while even still below,
the colors still at peak

defy the face of stone
turned icily their way.
When all the leaves are blown
and scattered all away,

the grasping trees will still
shake branches at the sky,
wishing it naught but ill,
the mountain in the sky.

What wrath the Chill winds show,
the seasons all at war,
the leaves against the snow,
and all of Haeth at war.

But mountain faces fall
before the things that grow.
The deepest roots of all
has the orchid in the snow.
