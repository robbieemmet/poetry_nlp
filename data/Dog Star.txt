Dog Star

I never cared much about stars,
but I loved fireflies.

I was so surprised to see them
in New Haven. I was hearing
that the summer night would someday be
without them.

Now? Now I have known what it is
to cry hot wax until the candles of my eyes
are stubs, low red flames
in a short and restless night.

I will bake ginger cookies all December.
I don't know if I will make people -
that's gone badly for me, now.
I will make dogs with stars for eyes.

They are watchers at the edge of firelight.
Even the ones without jobs have jobs -
to tell us they need us,
to tie us to the world

and help us love the stars.
