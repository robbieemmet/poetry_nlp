Isro, a Water God

Small and forgotten
doesn't mean powerless.

You should have checked the weather report
the moth brought before you left.

Wind is one god but this god is legion -
if the wind is the bow then the water is arrows.

Ice makes unsafe
the hot air balloon.
Rain obscures
the path ahead.

Who says war
has left Haeth?

Whether this god
wants it or not,
you're most likely dead.
