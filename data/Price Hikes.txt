Price Hikes

Gold isn't worth its weight now,
and you'll see no better shine
than a salmon or lake whitefish
caught on a fishing line.

Food and water and shelter,
these are the new currency,
and bonuses are the flotsam
that wash up from the sea.

Beauty is precious, expensive,
found in the sunset’s glow,
and the smell of moose meat roasting,
and the freshly fallen snow.

But you'd pay the most to go back
to before the schoolhouse burned,
before the lights erupted,
before the last season turned.
