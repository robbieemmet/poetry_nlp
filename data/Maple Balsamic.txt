Maple Balsamic

What makes me go chasing
that crazy flavor,
so bittersweet?

Bridge Square burns in my memory,
and a song I don't recognize
plays - one year ago.

Why do I seek
the songs I sought
in darker times and places?

Why do I yearn for maples
even though they're right outside,
just down the street?

I've learned,
in love and life,
to take the bitter with the sweet.

I am as mixed and tangled
as that bottle
from the town where I met her.

I have that Yankee sap in my veins.
She has the punchy sweetness
of Italian grapes.

May our love ferment a long time
in this sweet bottle.
I'm here to stay.
