La Rinconada

If the Himalayas are the roof of the world,
then this is the attic corner

where the darkness gathers most.
I used to think Hell was down. I was wrong.

These mountains are not the kind
you seek for enlightenment.

No, the metals of the world
are too heavy here.

The quicksilver and thin air
conspire to restrict inspiration

and the mind wanders
and the soul wanders,

not free, not even high,
just some tired bird like a pigeon,

living with humans
and carrying our letters and sins.
