A Trail Blessing

May the trail be even,
may your feet stay dry,
may your worries fly away
in a bluebird sky.

Life is rarely easy,
life is rarely fair,
but may you breathe easier
in Appalachian air.

You had an oath to follow,
and you played your part.
Now go enjoy the mountains,
let them heal your heart.

Let Springer be your springboard,
and I hope for you, my friend,
you find a good path forward
at the northern end.
