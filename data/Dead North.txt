Dead North

Good luck using a compass here,
and if you do, the ice is so awry,
tumbled uneven and severe,
you'd be a fool to do, or even try

what you set out at first to do:
prove that this place was worth its weight in cold.
If any north up here is true,
it's that the life up here is far too old

to be open to some great change,
some settlement or settling for a camp.
Can't sleep up here. The night's too strange,
dark through the day but lit up like a lamp.

The planet doesn't want you here.
Go scout around Mygeeto or Rhen Var.
At least they have an atmosphere
that doesn't hunt you like the fierce nekarr.
