The Pilgrim’s Vision

The entire horizon
looks like water.
Am I an island?

No, it’s only snow
deep between the trees,
the birches, the aspens

and the maples
calling to the wastes
to the west.

And north there’s more
still, more stillness
than even you need.

Permafrost.
Isn’t that what we all seek?
Stability, until instability.
