Bodge, the Underking

Under the kingdoms of Haeth,
the Underking hears all.
He undermines the city walls,
he underlies the royal halls,
he understands the rises and falls
of the kingdoms of Haeth.

He is the true king of Haeth,
the Underking of Truth.
He knows that power lies in strong roots
and hollow halls stuffed full of loot,
and once-small seeds that bear great fruit,
the one true king of Haeth.
