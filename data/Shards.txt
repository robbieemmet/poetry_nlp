Shards

Longer ago than the first long day,
a pot broke and spilled, its waters a sea.
The shards floated up, new islands of clay.
Hard people came there from the hard dark sea.

When the world ends in fire, the islands will crack.
The people will swim in the boiling deep.
The land will lay down on its aching back,
and the North Sea's last waves will sing it to sleep.
