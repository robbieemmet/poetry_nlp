Compline

I pray for the peace of those nights,
when crickets licked the wounds
of each hot day,

and each candle fell in love with a star
and each flame tried to caress them.
Every night there

felt like the first night after day was made,
when darkness was a joy that would fade,
not a shadow of a future not fulfilled.

They are stars, every one,
every accidental death, our unborn son.
We can look back into time and see them shine.

Let us sit out in the night,
between the candles and the stars,
their love and everlasting light.

Where love connects us,
nothing ever really dies.
We can fade into creation and be fine.
