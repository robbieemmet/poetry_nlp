The Cloven Hills

Are we protected from the sides
or we trapped inside
the place those rebels died

not long ago?

In the distance is a mountain.
Could that be the Floating Mountain?
If it was, were the dead mounting

an attack?

These hills divide the sense
of the word camp - sure, there are tents,
their walls are loose, their wires tense,

do they relax or do they stay
on watch? What kind of resting place are they?
The kind where you can walk away

when you are ready to advance?
