Winterkill

Imagine only Florida
or Baja Sur
being what they once were,
sun and sand, south wind,
low, gentle waves, and flowers,
and the North -

whatever phase,
steam, freeze, or deep sea,
the North has changed.

Water in all its forms
makes fools of mountains,
makes faces, laughs in fountains
and roars in storms.

This is the future,
this river of early winters.
I don't see anything
else anymore.
