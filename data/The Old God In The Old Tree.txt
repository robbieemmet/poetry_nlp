The Old God In The Old Tree

I will bring him gifts,
gifts to make his heart ache,
golden pears aplenty
for the old god in his tree.

May he dull the swords,
turn them into plowshares,
bury all the hatchets
underneath his watchful tree.

Let the seasons flow,
bringing peace and bounty.
May his garden ever grow,
the old god in his tree.
