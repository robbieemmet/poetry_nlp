The Boat That Would Not Sink

She was a former man'o'war,
retired into whaling.
She sailed around the Cape of Storms
without the need for bailing.

She snagged a whale outside Rocks Hole.
The whole ship might go under,
the captain thought, but she just rolled
and held onto her plunder.

Next she sailed away up north,
as north as north can go,
to seek the famous Straits of Wrath
among the endless snow.

She ran against the rough sea ice.
The crew thought she was lost,
but she tipped back, to their surprise,
and climbed the ice and crossed.

She's sailing out there somewhere now,
around the world, I think,
and written there across her bow:
ōThe Boat That Would Not Sink.ö
