Christmas in the Forestry Lookout

The night is cold and dark,
but here the air is warm.
My lonely little ark
rides out the solar storm.

I'm glad that I put up
these scavenged Christmas lights.
They help me to put up
with countless sleepless nights.

I came up from my base
early on Christmas Eve,
and here came face to face
with loot I could not leave,

for here there was a knife
and mountaineering rope.
For Christmas, I got life.
For Christmas, I got hope.
