Centralville

Where did the name come from
for this place on the margin,
here on the threshold

of meteorological fall
and paradoxical
winter wheat planting days?

This place has been overturned,
over and over.
Its roots are in the air.

But the people are resilient
like weeds.
But the thing about weeds

is they’re called that
when someone thinks
they’re not where they’re supposed to be.
