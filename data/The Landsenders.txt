The Landsenders

The sea feels different
when it's not a turquoise hot tub.

No coral reefs or dolphin swims
for these hard people:
this is an ocean
that eats you.

Even the lakes in these places
are much the same:

mysterious and deep,
where legends older
than ancient countries sleep
and early nights and winters

are nothing new.
I love this hard place and its people too.
