Broken Wheels

This crisis ended history.
The cycle's gone away.
The roads aren't safe for passage
and the dark creeps into day.

It's hard to argue history
was just a set of gears,
but if it was, the cycle's changed -
it isn't 80 years.

Now history's just story
carried singly by the soul.
The world must be remade anew
on forges fueled by coal.

The cars won't start, the road's too rough
to ever be safe again.
No turning but returning
to the laws of the first men.

In this new world of snow, the sled
will overtake the wheel.
There is no going back to that
if we ever want to heal.
