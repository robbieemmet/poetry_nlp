The World Ended and We Didn't Know It - Ushguli

Green as it ever was, the high pasture.
Deep as it ever was, the great snow.
How could you think we'd hear the signals
when we rarely get signals from below?

When the TV went down, we ignored it.
Losing the TV is nothing new.
Nobody comes here in the winter,
so it was spring before we knew.

We never had conquerors since we were settled.
We haven't had visitors yet this year.
The end of the world will rise like fire,
slowly, until it reaches here.
