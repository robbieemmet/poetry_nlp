First Light

Think now of the first light
at the dusk of Candlefeast,
symbolizing the first sunlight
ever glowing in the east.

It's a time for celebration
with your friends and gratitude,
but it's also for creation
if your party's in the mood.

Paint a painting of your party,
write a poem, write a song.
Have no fear if you're not arty -
you can't celebrate it wrong,

the lesson the sun imparted:
all the joy that art can bring.
Think how every day is started:
when the bugs see light, they sing.
