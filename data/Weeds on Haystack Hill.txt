Weeds on Haystack Hill

Why is revolution
seen as so remarkable?
What field doesn't need
to be overturned
to make room for new growth?

We are weeds -
we are everywhere, anyone.
We flock to disturbance.
We are not so easily disturbed.

We are the dust in your lungs
and the rain in your eyes.
This might be your land now,
but take your eyes off it -
you will see it was always ours.
