Aurora

What a gift it is
to see the midnight dawn,
to know the ones we lost
are never really gone.

Energy is not destroyed.
It is changed to something new,
ashes to violet auroras,
green, or rarely, blue.

I wish I could travel
in a warm ball of light,
feel alive in the glow
of the boreal night.

But even in darkness,
I have no fear.
Whether light or shadow,
the ones I lost are here.
