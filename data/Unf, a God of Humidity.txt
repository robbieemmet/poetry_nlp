Unf, a God of Humidity

On the sticky mornings of Bright
she is fog if you're lucky,
but not too much -

she delights
in your seeing
the sweat drip down your nose.

The gods
are not always causes -
some are witnesses,

and she is one of them,
floating through the damp air
while listless kith

drink cold berry juice,
wait in the shade,
and look forward to Breathe.
