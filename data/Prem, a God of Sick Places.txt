Prem, the God of Sick Places

It's not fair to say
that he's the one who makes places this way.
It's just whatever's present there, he's present too.

I don't think anyone knows
if he's a protector or he makes things worse,
but I don't think he makes these places cursed,

the long-neglected room,
the island on the isolated lake
where night means doom.

He is, in all these broken parts,
a certainty, a hope that all these places
have a chance to be

a place at all.
